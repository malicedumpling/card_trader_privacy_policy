# Privacy Policy for Card Trader Value Calculator

**Last Updated: January 24, 2026**

## Introduction

Card Trader Value Calculator ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use our Card Trader Value Calculator app.

## Information We Collect

### No Personal Data Collection
Our app does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal information, including but not limited to:
- Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics or tracking data
- Payment information

### Local Data Storage
The app stores only the following information locally on your device:
- **Calculation History**: Your calculation history is stored locally to provide a convenient reference for past calculations
- **User Preferences**: Settings such as buy/trade percentages and theme preferences are stored locally
- **App Settings**: Basic app configuration settings

All data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or third parties.

## How We Use Information

The locally stored data is used solely to:
- Provide calculation history functionality
- Remember your preferred settings between app sessions
- Maintain app functionality and user experience

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do **not** share, sell, rent, or disclose any information to third parties. Your data remains entirely on your device.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- We cannot access your data
- Data security depends on your device's security measures
- We recommend using device passcodes and biometric authentication

## Your Rights

You have full control over your data:
- **Delete Data**: Clear calculation history through the app's settings
- **Reset Preferences**: Reset all settings to default values
- **Uninstall**: Completely remove all data by uninstalling the app

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
